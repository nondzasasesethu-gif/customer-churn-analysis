# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

**Future Interns Data Science & Analytics – Task 2 (2026)**

A professional Power BI dashboard analyzing customer churn patterns for a telecom company, built using the Telco Customer Churn dataset.

---

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project identifies why customers leave a subscription-based telecom service and what actions can reduce churn. The analysis covers 7,043 customers across demographics, service types, contract types, and payment methods.

---

## 📁 Dataset

- **Source:** [Telco Customer Churn – Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/blastchar/telco-customer-churn)
- **Rows:** 7,043 customers
- **Columns:** 21 features including tenure, contract type, internet service, payment method, and churn status

## 💡 Key Insights

1. **Overall churn rate is 27%** — meaning 1 in 4 customers leaves the platform
2. **Month-to-month contracts have 42% churn** vs only 3% for two-year contracts
3. **Fiber optic customers churn at 42%** — the highest of any internet service type
4. **Electronic check payments** are associated with the highest churn rate
5. **New customers are most at risk** — churn drops significantly after the first 12 months
6. **Senior citizens churn more** (36%) compared to non-senior customers
7. **Gender has no significant impact** on churn — nearly equal between male and female

---

## ✅ Recommendations

1. **Incentivize long-term contracts** — offer discounts to move month-to-month customers to annual or two-year plans
2. **Investigate Fiber optic service quality** — high churn suggests dissatisfaction with service or pricing
3. **Encourage automatic payment methods** — customers on electronic check have higher churn; nudge them toward auto-pay
4. **Early retention program** — target customers in their first 12 months with loyalty rewards or check-in calls
5. **Senior citizen support** — offer dedicated support or simplified plans for senior customers
